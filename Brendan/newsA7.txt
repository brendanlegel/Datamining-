              <p class="speakable">A bounce house&nbsp;— with a&nbsp;9-year-old child inside —&nbsp;suddenly got swept&nbsp;onto a nearby highway in Southern California and slammed into a vehicle amid strong winds Saturday afternoon.</p>
              
                  <div class="ad-container tablet ad-h-250">
                      <div class="ad gpt" id="tablet-300x250"></div>

                  </div>
              
             
               
              
              
              


              
          
              <p class="speakable">Fortunately, the child survived with minor injuries.</p>
              
             
               
              
              
              


              
          
              <p>Deputies responded to the incident on Highway 395 in Adelanto just after 3 p.m.,&nbsp;the Victor Valley branch of the San Bernardino County Sheriff's Department said in a <a href="http://nixle.us/A548S" target="_blank">news release</a>.&nbsp;</p>
              
             
                           		
					<div class="ad gpt ad-h-1" data-ad-pos="inread" data-ad-size="1x1"></div>
              
              
              
              


              
          
              <p>The inflatable bounce house was swept away from a home in the area and rolled toward the highway with the child inside, investigators said. The bounce house hit a vehicle that was traveling southbound.&nbsp;</p>
              
             
               
              
              
              


              
          
              <p>The 9-year-old was taken to a hospital for treatment, while the driver of the vehicle that was hit was &quot;shook up from the ordeal&quot; but otherwise wasn't hurt.</p>
